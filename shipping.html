Shipping & Handling

On-line Shop: Shipping and handling charges are based on weight of the product, packing materials, processing time estimated for each order and delivery destination. Additional fees may be due for Canadian and other international destinations.
Shipping methods are -

U.S. Postal Service Parcel post for combined weights up to 1 1/2 lbs; estimated cost up to $7.00
U.S. Postal Service Priority Mail post for combined weights up to 10 lbs [restricted size - must fit into priority mail medium box 11" L x 8 1/2" W x 5 1/2" D]; cost at $14.35
UPS service for combined weights over 1 lb; commercial rates

Shipping options and calculated costs may be selected prior to your order Check Out.
